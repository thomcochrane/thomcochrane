Picasa



Dedicated image sharing repositories such as Flickr (http://www.flickr.com) and picasaweb (http://picasaweb.google.com) offer more interactive features than Vox's image repository, and are linkable to Vox and other Blogging systems. Direct mobile upload to Flickr can be achieved via either the Vox client, or email. Picasaweb mobile is supported via Shozu destination uploads.

Pedagogy:

Event, data and resource capturing and collaboration. Creativity.
