Photos

Almost all smartphones now include a built-in camera that is capable of capturing still images and video.

Most smartphones also include a built-in GPS (Global Positioning Service) that works via satellites to provide

longitude and latitude information for geo-tagging and geo-location. This facilitates Geo-tagging original photos,

and the ability to geolocate events on Google Maps, adding a location dimension to captured images and video.

Web 2 services that support geotagged photos include Flickr and Vox.
